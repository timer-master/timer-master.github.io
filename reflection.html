Reflect on Your Day

Reflecting is a powerful tool to ensure that you plan your day to the fullest. With just a quick reflection, you can determine if you managed your day well, and work to improve your time managment for tomorrow. Reflect on how you managed your time today by answering these quick questions below:

1. How did you manage your time today?


2. How did you plan your day well today?

2. How did your day go today?

2. How did your day go wrong today?


3. Plan ahead: How will you manage your time tomorrow?
